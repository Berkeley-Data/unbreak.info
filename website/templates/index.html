    Our mission is to break down barriers of communication and remove intentionally placed and reinforced biases people have against any particular group. We feel that when people connect and are given a chance to see each other they find they have a lot in common. And when people see each other as having the same hopes and dreams we begin to arrive at solutions for society.

    Combining cutting-edge data science with innovative grassroots organizing in our project Communication Unbreakdown, we have done just that.

    Our project has two main components, our software DARTS, and our Case Study working with People's Action.

    DARTS

    Dynamic and Responsive Targeting System. Optimize your target pool using a multi-armed bandit modified for delayed feedback scenarios.

    Case Study

    A case study using DARTS to find conflicted voters for People's Action's Deep Canvassing Campaign in the 2020 United States Presidential Election.
